03 · THE SYSTEM
What it handles

One AI powered system. Six core functions. Zero manual lift.

SwiftRoute runs the operational layer of your trucking business autonomously. Your role becomes oversight and approval, not execution.

01 live
Broker calls
Calls brokers, negotiates rates, and locks loads using a custom voice profile built for your operation.
02 live
Load matching
Pulls loads from load boards and matches them to your drivers based on lane, equipment, and availability.
03 live
Driver SMS
Two way SMS with drivers for status, ETA, and dispatch updates. No phone tag, no missed messages.
04 live
Check calls
Automated check calls and broker updates on schedule. Your phone stays quiet. Brokers stay informed.
05 live
Invoicing
Generates and sends invoices on delivery confirmation, then runs polite, persistent follow up on aging receivables.
06 live
Revenue tracking
Real time profitability per load, driver, and lane. The numbers you actually need, automatically.
